# Step 4.5 — Scenario info card

Scope: UI-only scenario brief panel.

Implemented:
- `Scenario info` button in the bedside monitor header
- collapsible card with scenario name, patient age/weight/sex/diagnosis, duration, description, and outputs/objectives when available
- scenario metadata cached from `/scenarios`

Purpose: keep clinical context available during a single-tab simulation without crowding the bedside monitor.
