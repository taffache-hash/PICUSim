# Not for clinical use

This software is an exploratory pediatric physiology simulation framework for education, model development, and hypothesis generation.

It is not a medical device. It is not validated for diagnosis, prescription, monitoring, triage, prognosis, or bedside decision support. It must not be used to guide clinical care.

The term “digital twin”, when present in internal filenames or development notes, is used as a developmental aspiration. The current software is not a validated patient-specific digital twin.

Outputs are generated by simplified semi-mechanistic and empirical modules. They may be physiologically plausible while still being wrong for a real patient.
